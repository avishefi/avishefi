### Avi Shefi

Software engineer. 12 years consulting on large-scale systems: high-throughput
backends, cloud infrastructure, and lately AI tooling and autonomous agents.

Most of the work is helping teams build systems that hold up under load, figure
out what's actually breaking, and make architecture calls with real constraints
attached. Some engagements are a single hard problem. Others run long enough to
watch the decisions compound.

I write about what comes out of that at [avishefi.com](https://avishefi.com):
architecture tradeoffs, decisions that stay stuck longer than they should, and
where things tend to go wrong.
